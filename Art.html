I am an Artist who paints in a realism form such as Landscape, Still life, Portraits, and Horses using an oil medium. Lately, I have enjoyed working in the genre of semi-abstract art. My work has been regularly shown in exhibitions with collecting many awards over the years. I love to explore my passion further by visiting different art museums and shows around the world . I am a talented art lover who loves to create and share my talent by attending many other art exhibitions.
